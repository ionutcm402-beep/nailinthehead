Privacy & Cookies

Last updated: June 2026

This policy explains what personal data Nail In The Head (“we”, nailinthehead.org) collects, why, and your rights under UK GDPR. We keep it short and plain.

1.Who we are

Nail In The Head is an independent digital business selling downloadable guides (“boxes”). For any privacy question, contact contact@nailinthehead.org. We are the data controller for the information described here.

2.What we collect

  • Analytics data — if you accept cookies, Google Analytics collects anonymous-ish usage data (pages visited, rough location, device/browser, how you found us). We use this only to understand how the site is used.
  • Your email — only if you choose to email us. We use it to reply to you.
  • Purchase data — when you buy a box, payment and order details are collected and processed by our store provider, Payhip, and the payment processors (PayPal / Stripe). We don't see or store your card details.

3.Cookies & your choice

Our site uses Google Analytics cookies to measure usage. These are not set unless you click Accept on our cookie banner. If you click Reject, no analytics cookies are placed and Google Analytics does not run. You can change your mind any time by clearing your browser storage for this site, which brings the banner back.

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies, and we do not sell your data to anyone.

4.Legal basis

For analytics, our legal basis is your consent (which you give via the cookie banner, and can withdraw). For replying to your emails and fulfilling purchases, the basis is our legitimate interest and the performance of a contract with you.

5.Who we share data with

We use trusted third parties who process data on our behalf: Google (analytics), Payhip (store & checkout), and PayPal / Stripe (payments). Each has its own privacy policy and GDPR compliance. We don't share your data with anyone else except where required by law.

6.How long we keep it

Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics' standard retention settings. Emails are kept only as long as needed to deal with your query. Purchase records are kept by Payhip as required for tax and accounting.

7.Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to have it corrected or deleted, to object to or restrict its processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these, email contact@nailinthehead.org. You also have the right to complain to the UK's Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8.International transfers

Some providers (such as Google) may process data outside the UK/EU. Where they do, they rely on safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses to keep your data protected.

9.Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted here.

This is a plain-language privacy notice for a small digital business. As you grow, or if you add more data collection, it's worth having it reviewed by a privacy professional.
