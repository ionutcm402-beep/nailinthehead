How it
works

You don't need to be a tech expert. If you can copy and paste, you can get brilliant results from AI.

1

Find the prompt you need

Browse our library by category — Work, Money, Letters, Everyday Life, Small Business, and Writing. Or use the search bar to find exactly what you need. Every prompt is written for a real, specific situation.

2

Copy the prompt

Click "Copy prompt" and the whole thing is on your clipboard. Every prompt has bits in [square brackets] — those are the parts you fill in with your own details. The more specific you are, the better the result.

3

Open any AI tool

Open any AI tool — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, or any other. Most are free to use. Paste your prompt into the message box and hit send.

4

Get your result

Within seconds you'll have a proper, tailored response. If you want to tweak it, just tell the AI what to change — "make it shorter", "make it more formal", "add a paragraph about X".
